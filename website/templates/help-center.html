FAQ

How do I purchase tickets for the zoo?

You can purchase tickets online through our website or at the ticket counters located at the entrance of the zoo.

What are the opening hours of the zoo?

The zoo is open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM every day, including weekends and public holidays.

Do you offer guided tours, and how can I book one?

Yes, we offer guided tours led by experienced zookeepers. You can book a tour through booking online on our website.

Can I bring food and drinks into the zoo?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed inside the zoo premises. However, we have various food outlets and dining areas where you can enjoy meals and refreshments.

What accommodation options are available at the zoo, and how can I book a stay?

We offer comfortable accommodation options within the zoo premises, including lodges and cabins. You can book a stay by contacting our reservations team or booking online through our website.
